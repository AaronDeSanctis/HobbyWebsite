Final Fantasy

My first experiance with final fantasy was with FF 8. I had never played a game as immersive and instantly rewarding as FF before and I was immediately hooked. I used every possible guide on gamefaqs.com to get every possible hidden item, character, or ability. I then played every other available FF I could find. From my experiance, video gaming was at it's height on the PS1 and PS2 consoles. Video games nowadays are either short and fun or long and boring. This line of games was both long and fun for about 10 or 11 games straight which is literally unheard of in the gaming world. My favorite game of all time is FF Tactics. The storyline was meaningful and the characters were unbeleivably deep and misunderstood. The plot had non-stop betrayal and back-stabbing scenerios with main characters which I feel makes for an unforgettable experiance.
