Mortal Kombat 9

I've been playing Mortal Kombat 9 since the 4th day after it was released in April 2010 on and off for 5 years now. I put in the disc for the first time and knew i was in love. The amount of control you have to win games is incredible. Which was a huge change from Call of Duty and League of Legends and World of Warcraft and tons of games where I feel like you can play to the highest extent of your ability and still lose games or matches based on existential circumstances. I play almost every character in the game, but I consistantly win more matches with Johnny Cage and Kung Lao. I have a training partner who I've been playing with for 4 years now. He is absolutely rediculous at the game. Pushing me to my limits every single match. I would say my greatest aspect as a player in Mortal Kombat is my overall fundamentals and my ability to read my opponents.
