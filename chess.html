Chess

The ancient game of Chess is both simple and complex simultaneously. I started playing when I was about 10 years old. I've never gone to any tournemants for the game but I probably could do well. I enjoy the fact that a single move can make or break the entire game at any moment. There's nothing like watching as your opponent looks at the board for about 5 minutes and moves in the wrong location. I've won a lot more matches off of my opponent's stupidity rather than my own cleverness. Which I don't like. I would much prefer to lose to a good player then win against an easy player. My greatest strength as a Chess player is my ability to take more pieces then my opponent while also playig safe with my own.
